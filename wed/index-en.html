The wedding will take place at the Antigua Biblioteca Almirall, on mount Tibidabo, just behind Barcelona.

The Biblioteca is a modernist building from the beginning of the XX century. It is dedicated to the memory of Valentí Almirall, a Catalan intellectual and politician who lived in the second half of the XIX century.
