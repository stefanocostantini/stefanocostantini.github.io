Writing

Here is a selection of my writing, available for you to download free of charge, in the format you prefer. Choose 'epub' for maximum compatibility with most e-readers, or 'mobi' if you have a Kindle device.

I've written a novel, 'Drifting Away', which focuses on the colonisation of Mars and the relationships between the humans that have made the journey and those that have stayed behind on Earth.

The other three books are short stories. 'Won't Catch on' is set in London during a flu pandemic (I wrote this in 2013!). 'Help.net' explores what happens when people rely too much on medical advice they get on the Internet (pre-AI, but I don't think it would be too different today). 'A London Holiday' is set in a near-future London.
